+++ type = "news" title = "Haiku nightlies site moved!" date = "2014-07-15T19:57:52.000Z" tags = ["nightlies"] +++ Effective immediately, the Haiku Nightlies page has moved from http://haiku-files.org/ to https://download.haiku-os.org/.
It was agreed that the nightly downloads location should be a bit more centralized around the main website domain. Oliver and Niels got busy setting up a new buildbot instance and the new nightly pages, and finished it sometime last week.

The "automatic update" repositories have also moved, they can now be (temporarily) found at https://download.haiku-os.org/haiku-repositories/. Sometime before the next release, these will likely be migrated to https://packages.haiku-os.org/, but this is lower priority for now.
