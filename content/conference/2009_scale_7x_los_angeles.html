SCaLE is the Southern California Linux Expo, an open source event that gathers open source communities, academic institutions and commercial exhibitors started in 2002. This event is supported by the presence of numerous open source projects, and is sponsored by prominent businesses such as IBM, Google and Dell. This is the third year in a row that Haiku has a presence SCaLE.
