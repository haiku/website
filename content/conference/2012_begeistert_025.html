BeGeistert is the traditional bi-annual meeting point for the international Haiku user and developer community. As usual, it'll be held in Düsseldorf, Germany.

Registration is now open at the BeGeistert.org website.
The BG-weekend plus Monday is 45 EUR including lunch. A night bed & breakfast in the youth hostel costs 27.50 EUR.
