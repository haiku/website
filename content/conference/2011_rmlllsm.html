2011 Libre Software Meeting is a free (as a beer and as a speech) and non commercial conference with talks, workshops and round tables about Free Software and its uses. The LSM goal is to provide a place for exchange between Free Software developers, users and stakeholders.

LSM will be held this year in Strasbourg from 9th to 14th of July, 2011 by a set of local LUGs and organizations with the help of several public and private sponsors.
