OpenSource World focuses on innovative solutions in real-world environments using open source, open standards and open architecture as part of an integrated IT infrastructure. The OpenSource World conference will present the latest Linux and open source ideas in a technical context by industry experts and innovators, giving IT end-users actionable initiatives that bring cost savings to the enterprise. Topics include Deployment and Management, Enterprise Application Infrastructure, Mobile, Security, Desktops and Netbooks, and Systems Troubleshooting. Haiku will be exhibiting at table #5 of the .org Zone.
