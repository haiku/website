The O'Reilly Open Source Convention, known as OSCON for short, is an annual convention for the discussion of free and open source software. Organized by O'Reilly Media and held each summer in the United States, OSCON also co-hosts other notable events, such as the Perl, PHP and Python conferences. This year is Haiku's debut at this conference.
