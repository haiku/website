BeGeistert is the traditional bi-annual meeting point for the international Haiku user and developer community. The week before the BeGeistert weekend, a coding sprint will be held and the following Monday there's a workshop for aspiring coders. All in the same location in Düsseldorf, Germany.

Planned Activities: 

As usual, there will be talks, workshops and presentations. There isn't yet an official program, besides confirmation that Clemens Zeidler and Christof Lutteroth fly in from Newzealand to present what's new with Stack&Tile and their other projects,

Attention:

  • The Coding Sprint will be in the week before the BeGeistert weekend, i.e. 17.- 22. October 2010.
  • The Workshop day will be held by Axel Dörfler on Monday, 25. October 2010. The workshop has to be booked separately from the BeGeistert weekend.
