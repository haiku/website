The eighth Free and Open source Software Developers' European Meeting (FOSDEM) is a two day event, organized by volunteers, to promote the widespread use of Free and Open Source software. Taking place in the beautiful city of Brussels, Belgium, FOSDEM meetings are recognized as "The best Free and Open Source events in Europe."
