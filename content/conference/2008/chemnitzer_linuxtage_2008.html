This is an event focused on free software that is held every year in the new lecture hall and seminar building of the Technical University of Chemnitz. The event consists of presentations, project stands, workshops, instalfests and a technology area. It is organized mainly by (former) students and employees of the Technical University of Chemnitz, with the cooperation of the Chemnitzer Linux User Group.
