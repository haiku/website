BeGeistert is the traditional bi-annual meeting point for the international Haiku user and developer community. The week after the BeGeistert weekend, a coding sprint will be held, all in the same location in Düsseldorf, Germany.

Registration is now possible at the BeGeistert.org website.
The BG-weekend will be 45 EUR including lunch. A night bed & breakfast in the youth hostel costs 27,50 EUR.

Planned Activities: 

The coding sprint will be from Monday to Friday in the week after the BeGeistert weekend.
As usual, expect talks, workshops and presentations. Currently there'll be talks on

  • debugging under Haiku (Ingo),
  • the Auckland Layout Editor (Clemens),
  • a development version of a new WonderBrush and its Qt Port (Stephan),
  • and classic BeOS apps (Adrien).
